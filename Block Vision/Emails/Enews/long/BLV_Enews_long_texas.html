How to avoid
getting "framed" by
an eyewear benefit.
When you select a vision plan, only to discover after the fact that the eyewear benefits don't deliver the true value that was promised, you've been "framed."

That's why it's so important to ask the right questions and to learn everything about the costs and features of a vision plan before you make a choice, not after.
Here are three things you can do to keep from
being "framed":
1. Inquire about how eyewear values are determined and who sets them. Find Out More.
Remember, it's a vision plan, so everything should be transparent.

Be skeptical of vision plans that offer you their own frame collection. When they set the value of frames in a collection, they can also inflate them as high as they want. This leaves you with no way of knowing the frame's real value.

Artificially inflated frame values create a false impression of a greater frame benefit. When a vision plan offers its own frame collection, ask the plan for the plan invoices.

Block Vision believes that full disclosure and complete transparency are the keys to selecting the best vision plan and that being fully informed is critical in determining a vision plan's true value.
2. Don't be misled when making an "apples-to-apples" comparison. Find Out More.
If you really want to determine the best value for your vision plan dollar, look beyond the premium when evaluating eyewear benefits. If your vision plan offers its own frame collection, you may not be getting the full value of your plan benefit.

Sometimes, when you pay less you get less.

Eyewear provided by your eye care doctor with real world pricing offers you the best overall value.
3. Be skeptical of vision plans that have an interest
in eyewear manufacturers. Find Out More.
Look for a vision plan that offers you maximum choice.

Block Vision doesn't have any interest in eyewear manufacturers, financial or otherwise. This enables us to offer you a vision benefits plan with virtually unlimited in-plan frame selection.
And because Block Vision firmly believes in freedom of choice, we don't place limits on where eyewear can be purchased either.

Finally, we let our extensive provider network "shop the market" for the best buys on all the latest frame styles.

All of this means you'll enjoy a vision plan with true value and savings at a fair price.
