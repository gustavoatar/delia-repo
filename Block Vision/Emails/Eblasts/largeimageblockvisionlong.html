Don't let the seemingly high frame allowance of a limited-collection fool you
into believing that the "savings" of a vision plan's eyewear benefit
is a good value.

Inquire about how in-plan eyewear prices are determined and who sets them.
Be skeptical of vendors who offer you a limited eyewear collection. When they
set the value of frames in a collection they can also inflate them as high as
they want, leaving you no way of knowing what their real value is.

Artificially inflated frame values create a false impression of a greater frame
benefit. When a vision plan offers its own frame collection, ask the plan for the
frame invoices.

Eyewear with real world pricing, provided by your eye care doctor, offers you
the best overall value.
