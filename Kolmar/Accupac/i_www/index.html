Welcome to Acupac Packagin, inc.

A division of Kolmar Labs Group

Acupac is a full service contract manufacturer of dissolvable films, Gels, packettes, thermoforms, dosed non-wovens and dry-coated non-wovens. Our services include bulk formulation, product design/development, distribution and sampling.

We engineer and build equipment to meet product design requirements, and offer on-site testing capabilities.
